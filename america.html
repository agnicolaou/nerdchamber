NeRd  AcRosS  AMEriCA!


Nerd Chamber is on a mission from God to spread its love virus across America!

The goal is to hang and permanently display one art piece in a public establishment (bar, restaurant, oddities shop, music venue, coffee shop, etc.) in each of the 50 states.

The catch? Beside the fact that it's fun and it makes Nerd Chamber artist, A. Nic feel like he's hot shit...this is a great way for your establishment to get some unique artwork on its walls for FREE while spreading awareness for our brand!

In addition to free artwork, approved businesses will also receive:

  • A marker on our digital map (above) displaying your business location, address info, and will direct users to your website when clicked.
  • A post about your business on social media (over 10k followers).
  • And did we mention FREE art for your lovely patrons to enjoy?
